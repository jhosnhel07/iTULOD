About iTULOD

Built for how Ilocanos actually get around.

From the tricycle down your street in Laoag to a same-day parcel across Ilocos Norte, iTULOD brings every trip and delivery into a single, transparent platform — for riders, customers, and the admins who keep it running.

Verified riders

Every rider is screened — license, OR/CR, and vehicle details reviewed by our admin team before they accept a single booking.

Upfront fares

See your estimated fare before you confirm — based on real base rates and per-kilometer pricing for each vehicle type.

Rated by the community

Every completed trip and delivery can be rated, so quality service is always visible and rewarded.
